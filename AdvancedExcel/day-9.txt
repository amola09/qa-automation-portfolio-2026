Filter: Allows users to display only selected data from a worksheet while hiding the rest of the data temporarily.
It helps in viewing and analyzing specific information without deleting any data.


Steps to Apply Filter:
1. Select data
2. Click on "Home" Tab ==> Click on "Sort & Filter" ==> Select "Filter"
